Task
You draw 2 cards from a standard 52-card deck without replacing them. What is the probability that both cards are
of the same suit?
=>

4 Suit. (Diamond, Heart, Clover, Spade)

First Card Draw => 13/52 = 1/4
Second Same Card Draw => 12/51

Then, 1/4 * 12/51 = 3/51.

There are 4 Suit, 3/51 * 4 = 12/51
